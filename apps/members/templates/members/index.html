Welcome to Dozo

Welcome to Dozo, a data-driven productivity, collaboration, and goal targeting tool.

Why Join?

We all have different methods and means of progress in our journeys to becoming self-sufficient developers,
but there are two main determining factors for our success: time and efficiency.

With Dozo, here's how you can optimize your growth as a developer:

  • Visualize your hours spent at the Dojo or wherever you're able to find time to code.
  • Set personal goals for each day and work towards accomplishing them before the day ends.
  • Track your progress and update others on where you are in your learning track.
  • Stay connected with cohort-mates and others at the Dojo so all can grow together.
