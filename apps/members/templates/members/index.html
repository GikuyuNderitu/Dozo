Welcome to Dozo

Welcome to Dozo, a IoT-powered, data-driven productivity, collaboration, and goal targeting tool.

We all have different methods and means of progress in our journeys to becoming self-sufficient developers,
but there are two main determining factors for our success: time and efficiency.

With Dozo, here's how you can optimize your growth as a developer:

  • Visualize your hours spent at the Dojo or wherever you're able to find time to code
  • Set personal goals for each day and work towards accomplishing them before the day ends
  • Track your progress and update others on where you are in your learning track
  • Check in and stay connected with cohort-mates through virtual stand-up meetings

How Dozo Works

In general, Dozo is about keeping it simple:

  • Sign up for IFTTT and connect it to your device and this website
  • When you arrive at the Dojo, Dozo will log your start of day
  • Take a minute to jot down your day's goals -- they'll show up on the newsfeed
  • Check off each goal and that'll be up on the wall too
  • Check your personal dashboard to see your time, progress, and efficiency score
  • Visit the assignments pages to read what others are posting about each assignment
