Welcome to Dozo

Welcome to Dozo, a data-driven productivity, collaboration, and goal targeting tool.

Why Join?

We all have different methods and means of progress in our journeys to becoming self-sufficient developers, but there are two main determining factors for our success: time and efficiency.

With Dozo, you can optimize your growth as a developer by:

  • Visualize your hours spent at the Dojo or wherever you're able to find time to code.
  • Set personal goals for each day and work towards accomplishing them before the day ends.
  • Track your progress and update others on where you are in the learning platform.
  • Stay connected with your cohort-mates and others at the Dojo so that everyone can grow together.
